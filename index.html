What services does SphereRoot offer?

We create websites, digital identities, visual systems, and strategic brand assets tailored for modern businesses.

Do you handle both design and development?

Yes — from visual concept to functional execution, everything is handled under one structure.

How many requests can I make?

As many as you like — with a subscription, you can queue unlimited requests, and they’ll be handled one at a time in priority order.

What types of design do you handle?

Websites, product UI, landing pages, brand assets, decks, social media visuals — anything digital that needs to look and feel sharp.

What tools do you use?

We use the right tool for the right problem — from design boards and development stacks to strategy systems and a little healthy obsession with detail.

Can I pause the subscription?

Yes — you can pause anytime and resume when you’re ready. Unused days roll over.

How long does a project usually take?

Depending on scope, most projects are delivered within 1–3 weeks.
